## Purpose

This folder contains the internal audit program, schedules, and audit reports for the CloudVault FHX ISMS. ISO 27001 Clause 9.2 requires the organization to conduct internal audits at planned intervals to provide information on whether the ISMS conforms to the organization's own requirements and to the requirements of ISO/IEC 27001:2022. Internal audits must be conducted by qualified auditors who are independent of the area being audited.

## Annual Audit Coverage Plan

The CloudVault FHX internal audit program covers the full ISMS scope over a rolling 12-month cycle:

| Quarter | Audit Focus | ISO 27001 Clauses | Key Annex A Controls |
|---|---|---|---|
| Q1 (Jan-Mar) | Leadership and risk management | 4, 5, 6 | 5.1-5.8, 5.15-5.18 |
| Q2 (Apr-Jun) | Operational controls and supplier management | 8 | 5.19-5.22, 8.1-8.15 |
| Q3 (Jul-Sep) | Technological controls and incident management | 8 | 5.24-5.28, 8.16-8.34 |
| Q4 (Oct-Dec) | Performance evaluation and continual improvement | 9, 10 | All Annex A residual |

## Audit Finding Classifications

| Classification | Definition | Resolution Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Major Nonconformity | Complete absence of a required process or control, or systematic failure | 30 days |
| Minor Nonconformity | Isolated failure or gap that does not invalidate the control | 90 days |
| Observation | Potential weakness not yet a nonconformity | Next review cycle |
| Opportunity for Improvement | Suggestion for enhancing effectiveness beyond requirements | At management discretion |

## Relationship to FedRAMP Assessment

The internal audit program complements the annual FedRAMP 3PAO assessment. The internal audit focuses on ISMS management system conformance: are we following our own procedures, are policies current, are roles assigned. The 3PAO assessment focuses on technical control effectiveness: are the controls actually working. Together they provide a complete picture of ISMS health.
