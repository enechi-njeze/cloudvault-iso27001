## Purpose

This folder contains the Information Security Policy suite for the CloudVault FHX ISMS. ISO 27001 Clause 5.2 requires top management to establish an information security policy that is appropriate to the organization's purpose, includes information security objectives, commits to satisfying applicable requirements, and commits to continual improvement of the ISMS.

Supporting policies cascade from the top-level policy and provide specific direction on each major security domain. All policies must be reviewed at least annually and updated following any significant change to the system, threat environment, or regulatory requirements.

## Policy Hierarchy

The CloudVault FHX policy framework follows a three-tier structure. The top-level Information Security Policy sets the mandate and commitment from senior leadership. Domain-specific policies provide operational direction for each security area. Procedures and work instructions provide step-by-step implementation guidance for specific tasks.

This hierarchy ensures that a policy reviewer at any level, whether a certification auditor, the AO, or a new employee, can trace any security requirement from the governing regulation down to the specific operational procedure that satisfies it.

## Policy Review Requirements

Under ISO 27001:2022, all policies must be reviewed at planned intervals (at least annually) and whenever significant changes occur. Each policy document must show its version, review date, approver, and next review date. Policies that are overdue for review are a minor nonconformity finding in an ISO 27001 certification audit.
